MAKER

Chain prompts and pipeline steps as a visual graph, then compile the wired order into one prompt.

  • Add nodes from the left palette — each lands in the center of your view.
  • Wire by dragging from a node's right port ▸ onto another node's left port.
  • Move nodes by their header. Pan the canvas by dragging empty space. Zoom with the scroll wheel.
  • Cut a wire by clicking it. Delete a node with its × button.
  • Lock nodes inject HARDLOCK PLOCK onto the compiled prompt. Note nodes never compile.
  • Compile walks the graph in wired order; Export / Import save and reload a flow as JSON.
